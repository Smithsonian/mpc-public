# Edgar Wilson Award Winners

Information regarding the Edgar Wilson Award can be found on the [Award Information](edgar-wilson-award-information.md) page.

The following table lists the recipients of the Edgar Wilson Award for the annual periods from 11 June 2018 through 11 June 2024. As described on the [Award Information](edgar-wilson-award-information.md) page, the terms of the Edgar Wilson bequest were changed such that as of October 2020, no financial awards would be made to award winners, but they would be honored with a commemorative plaque.

    Donald Machholz,
    Shigehisa Fujikawa,
    Masayuki Iwamoto

- **C/2018 Y1 (Iwamoto)**
